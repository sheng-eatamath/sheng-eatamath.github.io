Sheng Zhang

I am a research assitant at MBZUAI (Mohamed Bin Zayed University of AI) advised by Prof. Kun Zhang. Before that I was a master student at MBZUAI advised by Prof. Salman Khan and co-advised by Prof. Zhiqiang Shen. I obtained my bacholar degree in Software Engineering at Tongji University in China.

My primary research interests lie in learning visual concepts within open-world contexts, based on the principles of self-supervised learning and causality. My goal is to generalizing to novel visual concepts as well as understanding their physical properties/relationships, with few human annotations, in various computer vision tasks.
