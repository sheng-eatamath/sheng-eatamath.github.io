Sheng Zhang

I am a research assitant at MBZUAI (Mohamed Bin Zayed University of AI) advised by Prof. Kun Zhang (from CMU). Before that I was a master student at MBZUAI advised by Prof. Salman Khan and co-advised by Prof. Zhiqiang Shen. I obtained my bacholar degree in Software Engineering at Tongji University in China.

My primary research interests lie in learning visual concepts within the open-world contexts, based on self-supervised learning, latent-variable modeling, and causality. My goal is generalizing to novel visual concepts as well as understanding their physical properties/relationships, with few human supervision.
